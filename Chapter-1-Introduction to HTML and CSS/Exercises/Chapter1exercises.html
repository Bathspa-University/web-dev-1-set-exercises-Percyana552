Orange cake

This is a simple and appetizing cake, reciepe was made in Portugal.

ingredients

  • 6 Eggs(yolks and whites seperated)
  • 3/4 cup of butter
  • 2 and 1/2 cups of sugar
  • 2 and 1/2 cups of flour
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • Juice and zest from 1 Orange
  • Topping
  • Juice from 2 oranges
  • 1/4 cup of sugar

Directions

  1. In large bowl, add the butter ,
    sugar and mix until the sugar dissolves.
  2. Add the egg yolks, stirring well,
    than add the orange juice and zest
  3. Sift the flour with the baking powder
    and add to the previous preparation.
  4. Beat the egg whites until,
    it forms peaks and mix in carefully.
  5. Put in a greased and dusted with flour cake pan,
    and bake at 180c/355F for about 40 minutes
  6. Topping
  7. Mix the juice of the 2 oranges with the 50 grams of the sugar,
    until the sugar dissolves completely
    and add it over the cake when the cake is cool
